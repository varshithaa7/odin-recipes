LASAGNA

home

DESCRIPTION

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheese, baked to perfection.

INGREDIENTS

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef or chicken (or vegetables for veg option)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

STEPS

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles as per the package instructions and drain them.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté chopped onions and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef (or vegetables) and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce, add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, meat sauce, and ricotta mixture.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, topping with mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
