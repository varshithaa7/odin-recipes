PANINI FROM PARIS

DESCRIPTION

A grilled Italian sandwich made with ciabatta or baguette, filled with meats, cheeses, and vegetables, toasted until crispy and warm.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ciabatta or baguette (cut in half)
  • 2 slices mozzarella or cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices ham, turkey, or grilled vegetables (for veg option)
  • 2 tbsp pesto or mayonnaise
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 1 handful spinach or arugula
  • 1 tbsp butter (for grilling)

STEPS

  1. Slice the ciabatta or baguette in half.
  2. Spread pesto or mayonnaise on the inside of the bread.
  3. Layer with cheese, ham (or veggies), tomato slices, and spinach.
  4. Close the sandwich and lightly butter the outside.
  5. Grill in a panini press or on a stovetop pan until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy.
