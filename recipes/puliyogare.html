puliyogare

DESCRIPTION

A South Indian tamarind rice dish, tangy and spicy, made with tamarind paste, sesame seeds, jaggery, and tempered spices.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 3 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp jaggery (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds, roasted and ground
  • 1 tsp puliyogare spice mix (optional)
  • Salt to taste

STEPS

  1. Cook rice and allow it to cool.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, and peanuts.
  3. Add dried red chilies and curry leaves, and sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Pour in the tamarind paste, jaggery, turmeric, and salt. Cook until thick.
  5. Add the roasted sesame powder and mix well.
  6. Mix the tamarind sauce with the cooled rice and combine thoroughly.
  7. Let it sit for a few minutes for flavors to blend before serving.
